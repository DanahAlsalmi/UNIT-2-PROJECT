Al Masjed Al Haram, the greatest Islamic spot on earth.

Al Masjed Al Haram receives great care, using the finest materials and innovative technologies to serve pilgrims, whose number exceeds millions per year. Over 24 hours, every day of the year, tranquility dominates the souls of visitors to Al Masjed Al Haram in Makkah, and preachers and worshipers are found in every corner of it, as they visit the place with sincere hope and overwhelming longing.

Hira Cultural District, the most famous tourist district of Makkah.

Hira Cultural District, located next to the famous landmark of Makkah, Jabal Al-Nour, provides a uniquely enriching experience, in a cultural, historical and entertaining journey to the worlds of the past, to remain alive in memory. The area of the district is estimated at 67,000 square meters.

The Cosmology Research Center (the museum) consists of four floors, and the implementation work is in an advanced stage. First floor: Dedicated to showcasing everything related to the Makkah Clock Tower, including its construction and the technologies used to determine time. Additionally, there is a terrace that overlooks the Kaaba. Second floor: It is dedicated to everything related to time measurement, human inventions, and documenting their attempts to know the time. Third floor: It is dedicated to the sun, earth, moon, and related topics. Fourth floor: It is dedicated to learning about the universe in general, including stars, constellations, and astronomical phenomena.
